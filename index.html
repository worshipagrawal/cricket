Welcome to CRICKET 11

Rules Are as Follows:

  • -Both users take turns to get a player after starting the game.
  • -Players have skill points that convert to team's runs.
  • -Batsmen positions: 1-4, all-rounders: 5-7, bowlers: 8-11.
  • -Batsmen earn runs in batting positions, zero runs in bowling positions.
  • -Bowlers earn runs in bowling positions, zero runs in batting positions.
  • -Batsmen and bowlers in all-rounder positions earn half their skill points as runs.
  • -All-rounders earn runs in any position.
  • -The team with the most runs after 11 turns wins.
